ABOUT US

A new face in Le Blanc-Mesnil, Padrino is a fast-casual* dining concept offering a varied culinary experience made with fresh produce. We place great importance on the quality of everything we serve. The freshness of our ingredients is at the heart of our values — and because your satisfaction matters to us just as much, we make sure every dish is packed with flavour. On the menu: pizzas, pastas, tex-mex, paninis, salads, fresh drinks and desserts.

We want our restaurant to be a place of warmth and conviviality where you can enjoy our generous, homemade cooking in a relaxed, friendly and family atmosphere. Whether you come as a family, with friends, as a couple or on your own, the Padrino team takes pride in welcoming and serving you with pleasure and warmth, 7 days a week from 11AM to 11PM.

Halfway between fast food and a sit-down restaurant, "fast-casual" is a premium fast-food concept. In other words, Padrino welcomes you at the counter and serves you quickly — like any fast-food outlet — but offers higher-quality products in a carefully curated atmosphere worthy of a full table-service experience.

Come and discover the delicious flavours of our pizzeria in Le Blanc-Mesnil. With fresh, top-quality ingredients, our menu features a variety of traditional and original pizzas to suit all tastes. Order online or come and visit us for an unforgettable dining experience. We are the ideal pizzeria for pizza lovers looking to enjoy handcrafted pizzas made with care.
